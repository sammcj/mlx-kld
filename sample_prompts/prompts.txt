Method get_or_set(self, key, factory, ttl=None) is the headline API ; it tries get first under self._lock, returns hit if present and not expired, otherwise releases the lock, calls value = factory(key) outside the lock to avoid blocking other callers during a slow upstream, then re-acquires the lock and calls self.set(key, value, ttl=ttl) ; on factory exception it caches a sentinel _NEGATIVE = object() with negative=True and the negative_ttl, then re-raises.
